Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a reverse GST calculator?

A reverse GST calculator is a function that determines the original base price and the tax amount from a final price that already includes GST. On our tool, this is the 'Include GST' mode. It's essential for creating tax invoices when you only have the total amount paid by a customer.

How does the 'including GST calculator' feature work?

The including GST calculator feature works by reversing the standard tax calculation. For instance, if you enter ₹118 with a GST rate of 18%, our tool correctly calculates the Net Amount as ₹100 and the GST Amount as ₹18. It uses the formula: `Base Amount = Total Amount / (1 + (GST Rate / 100))`.
